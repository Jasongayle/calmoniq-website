Privacy isn't a feature. It's the foundation.

Most money apps want your bank login and store your finances on their servers. Calmoniq does the opposite.

Stays on your device

Your data is a single encrypted file (AES-256). Keep it on your computer, in Dropbox/OneDrive, or on a USB drive. Your choice.

No account, works offline

There's nothing to sign into and nothing to leak. Calmoniq runs fully offline after you activate your license once.

You can delete it all

No server copy exists. Delete the file and it's gone. Export to PDF or CSV anytime.
