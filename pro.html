Questions

Is my clients' data uploaded anywhere?

No. Client records and reports are stored in an encrypted file on your computer. The only network activity is licence activation and update checks.

Does it work on Mac and Windows?

Yes — Calmoniq Professional is the same desktop app, on macOS and Windows.

Are the calculations accurate for Canada?

Yes — Canadian terminology and rules (GDS/TDS, stress test, land transfer tax, TFSA/RRSP/FHSA). All figures are estimates; final mortgage approval always depends on the lender, insurer, credit, income verification, and underwriting.

Can I put my own logo and disclaimer on reports?

Yes. Upload your logo, set your brand colour, contact details, footer, and a custom disclaimer. They appear on every report.

Do you offer help setting up my branding?

Yes. Pro is self-serve, and an optional $199 branding setup adds done-for-you logo, colours, and report-template setup plus help preparing your first sample reports.

Is it a subscription?

Calmoniq Pro is an annual licence, which covers ongoing Canadian tax-rule updates and support. (Just need personal planning? Calmoniq Personal is a one-time lifetime licence.)
