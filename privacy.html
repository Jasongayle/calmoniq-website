Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Calmoniq is built privacy-first. Your financial information stays in an encrypted file on your own device. We do not have a copy of it, and we cannot see it.

Your financial data

Everything you enter in the App — income, accounts, balances, property details, notes — is stored only in an encrypted file (AES-256) on your computer or wherever you choose to keep it (for example a Dropbox or OneDrive folder, or a USB drive). It is never uploaded to us. We have no account system and no server that holds your financial data, so there is nothing for us to lose, sell, or hand over.

What does leave your device

The App connects to the internet only in these specific cases:

  • Licence activation: when you activate your licence key, the key is sent to our payment provider (Lemon Squeezy) to confirm it is valid. This happens once; afterward the App works offline.
  • Update checks: the App periodically checks our public releases page (GitHub) to see if a newer version is available. This sends only a standard web request, not your data.
  • AI assistant (optional): if you choose to connect your own AI provider key, your questions and a summary of your financial data are sent directly to that provider (for example OpenAI, Anthropic, or Google) using your key. This only happens if you turn it on, and your key is stored encrypted on your device. If you use the local Ollama option, nothing leaves your computer.

No tracking or analytics

The App contains no analytics, advertising, telemetry, or tracking. We do not profile you or your usage.

Payments

Purchases are handled by Lemon Squeezy, our payment provider and merchant of record. They process your payment and contact details under their own privacy policy. We receive order information (such as your email and licence key) so we can support you.

Newsletter

If you subscribe to our newsletter, we use your email address only to send the newsletter and the guide you requested, with your express consent, as required by Canada's anti-spam law (CASL). Every email includes an unsubscribe link, and you can opt out at any time.

Your control

  • Export: you can export your data to PDF or CSV from within the App at any time.
  • Backup: use File → Back Up Now or Save a Copy to keep encrypted copies.
  • Delete: to delete everything, simply delete your data file (and any backups). There is no server copy to remove.

Children

The App is intended for adults managing their own finances and is not directed at children under 13.

Changes

If we change this policy, we will update this page and the "last updated" date.

Contact

Questions about privacy: support@calmoniq.com.
